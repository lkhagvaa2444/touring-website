Meg's Guest House

Meg’s Guesthouse is the perfect starting point for a Mongolia adventure.
We have soft beds, a small kitchen for those early mornings and a communal space to chat with fellow travelers and find all those special destinations you only get through word of mouth. If your hear for a little longer or prefer more privacy we got you covered with single rooms, double rooms and even 1-3 rooms apartments. If you want the convenience of being in the center of the city, the comfort of a warm bed, the information to plan any trip and go anywhere, all at an affordable price, then Megs Guesthouse is for you!
