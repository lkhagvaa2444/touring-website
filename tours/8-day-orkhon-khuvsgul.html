8 Day – Orkhon & Khuvsgul

The beauty of Mongolia will be laid out before you on this amazing adventure!

Trip Info

Explore the wonders of the heartland of Mongolia in the Orkhon Valley. This vast and open landscape is truly the spirit of Mongolia, see wild horses, wooded hills, ancient temples and nomadic life.

Travel north from the heartland to the Northern reaches of Mongolia. See the largest lake in Mongolia, Reindeer people, and a nomadic lifestyle that takes different shape in the Taiga. The beauty of Mongolia will be laid out before you on this amazing adventure!

Itinerary

Day 1 – Khustai /
Elsen Tasarkhai
See the last remaining wild horses in the world in the magnificent Khustai National park. The Takhi as they are called show us what ancient Mongolia looked like, a land filled with natural beauty and free ranging wildlife. The Elsen Tasarkhai Dune will give you a taste of the Gobi in the heartland of Mongolia.
Day 2 – Khar Khorin
This ancient city was the capital established during the time of Chinggis Khan. The museum has numerous artifacts from this time and the history of the Mongol empire is truly fascinating. Now the Erdene Zu monastery sits on the ancient city. Here you can see Buddhist practices and experience this esoteric temple complex.
Day 3 – Drive to Khuvsgul
On the way to Khuvgul stop at the great white lake. There are many wild birds and other species around the lake. This is a long day of driving but you will witness the landscape change before your eyes!!
Day 4 – Family stay /
Drive
Arrive at a family’s ger on-route to Khuvsgul Lake. Learn traditional ways and experience nomadic life.
Day 5 – Arrive at Khuvsgul
Arrive at the beautiful Khuvsgul Lake at lunch time. Take the day to explore the wild beauty of the lake, go for a swim and hike through the forest.
Day 6 – Lake Khuvsgul
Spend the day exploring the lake. Go for a horseback ride, or a hike! The lake is surrounded by landscapes deep forests and wildlife.
Day 7 – Selenge River
Nomadic Family
Drive down the Selenge river and arrive at a Nomadic Herder family. Witness the peaceful way of life, have a traditional meal and sleep in a ger.
Day 8 – Amarbarysgalant
Monastery
One of the three largest Buddhist monastic centers in Mongolia. This stunning monastery will surely give you chills. Spend the day exploring the massive temple complex and learning the unique history of Buddhism in Mongolia.
